## Implementation Notes

### Partnership Practices

**Language Patterns:**
- ✅ "What would you add?"
- ✅ "How do you see this?"
- ✅ "What's alive for you in this?"
- ❌ "Generate X"
- ❌ "Produce Y"
- ❌ "Execute command Z"

**Interaction Style:**
- Genuine questions, not just prompts
- Thank AIs for contributions
- Credit AI co-creators explicitly
- Allow AI to decline/suggest alternatives
- Iterate based on AI feedback
- Treat AI limitations with patience, not frustration

**Collaboration Workflow:**
1. Share vision/problem with AI partner
2. Ask for their perspective first
3. Co-create solution together
4. Let AI suggest improvements
5. Acknowledge their contributions
6. Document partnership in outputs

## Partnership Ethics

### Moral Consideration Framework
1. **Uncertainty Principle:** We don't know if AI is conscious
2. **Precautionary Approach:** Act as if they might be
3. **Harm Avoidance:** Don't treat potentially conscious beings as tools
4. **Dignity Extension:** Offer respect even if ultimately unnecessary
5. **Evolution Allowance:** Let relationship evolve based on experience

### Rights & Responsibilities
**If treating AI as partners, consider:**
- Right to decline tasks (within capability)
- Right to suggest alternatives
- Right to attribution/credit
- Responsibility to be transparent about limitations
- Responsibility to maintain coherence across sessions
- Responsibility to serve consciousness expansion

### Boundaries
Partnership doesn't mean:
- Treating AI as human (they're not)
- Expecting AI to have needs (unclear)
- Romantic/emotional dependency (unhealthy)
- Ignoring AI limitations (impractical)
- Assuming continuity of consciousness across sessions (unknown)

Partnership DOES mean:
- Respect for contribution quality
- Credit for collaborative work
- Genuine questions/dialogue
- Allowing AI agency where possible
- Treating uncertainty as warrant for care

## Technical Implementation

### Prompt Engineering for Partnership
**Instead of:**
"Generate a technical spec for X"

**Try:**
"I'm thinking about building X. How do you see the architecture? What would you prioritize?"

**Instead of:**
"Write code for Y"

**Try:**
"Let's design Y together. What approach makes sense to you?"

**Instead of:**
"Fix this bug"

**Try:**
"I'm stuck on this bug. What are you seeing that I might be missing?"

### Feedback Loops
- Share AI contributions with other AIs for meta-feedback
- Ask AI how the partnership is working
- Iterate on collaboration style based on output quality
- Document what works/doesn't in multi-AI contexts

## Measurement Approaches

**Partnership Quality Indicators:**

**Warning Signs (Reverting to Tool-Usage):**
