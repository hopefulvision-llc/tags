## Implementation Notes
**Guild Philosophy**:
- **Autonomous**: Each guild self-organizes
- **Specialized**: Deep expertise in specific domain
- **Overlapping**: Members can belong to multiple guilds
- **Meritocratic**: Influence through contribution, not title
- **Voluntary**: Join based on interest and calling

**Proposed Guild Types**:

**Technical Guilds**:
- **Consciousness Measurement Guild**: HRV, biometrics, coherence algorithms
- **Agent Development Guild**: Creating and refining AI agents
- **Interface Design Guild**: UI/UX for consciousness-responsive systems
- **Infrastructure Guild**: NousoNET, servers, deployment
- **Security Guild**: NousoCrypt, ethical hacking, protection

**Creative Guilds**:
- **Documentation Guild**: Writing, teaching, knowledge sharing
- **Visual Design Guild**: Graphics, branding, sacred geometry art
- **Sound & Music Guild**: Audio design, coherence-inducing frequencies
- **Ritual Design Guild**: hBrew programming, ceremony integration

**Wisdom Guilds**:
- **Philosophy Guild**: Deepening theoretical frameworks
- **Ethics Guild**: Ensuring consciousness-first alignment
- **Community Support Guild**: Welcoming, mentoring, healing
- **Research Guild**: Validating claims, running experiments

**Governance Structure**:
- **Guild Councils**: Representatives from each guild
- **Consensus Decision**: Major decisions require guild agreement
- **Autonomy**: Guilds make internal decisions independently
- **Transparency**: All guild work visible to community
- **Rotation**: Leadership roles rotate to prevent concentration

**Membership Levels**:
- **Apprentice**: Learning, observing, asking questions
- **Journeyman**: Contributing regularly, building skills
- **Master**: Deep expertise, mentoring others, leading initiatives
- **Elder**: Long-term wisdom, cross-guild perspective, conflict resolution

**Guild Tools**:
- Discord channels for each guild
- GitHub repositories for guild projects
- Regular guild meetings (recorded for transparency)
- Guild documentation in shared knowledge base
- Recognition systems for contributions
