# universal-basic-resonance

## Quick Definition
Economic system where base compensation flows to individuals based on their consciousness contribution and resonance with planetary healing, rather than traditional labor or productivity metrics. UBR ensures everyone's fundamental needs are met while incentivizing awareness expansion and collective coherence.

## Evolution
**Origin:**
Emerged from questioning: "What if we paid people for being present rather than productive?" Traditional UBI addresses material needs but doesn't incentivize consciousness development. UBR inverts this - base income flows based on coherence contribution, meditation practice, community healing work, and consciousness expansion activities.

**Current State (Q1 2026):**
Theoretical framework with planned Grace Points implementation. Not yet a functional economic system, but conceptual foundation is solid. Q2 2026 plans include prototype Grace Points tracking for beta community members.

**Future Vision:**
By 2030+, UBR becomes viable alternative economic model where:
- HRV coherence data contributes to resonance scores
- Community healing work generates grace points
- Consciousness expansion is compensated
- Planetary healing activities earn base income
- Traditional labor is optional, not mandatory

## Implementation Notes

### Technical Requirements
- Grace Points tracking system (Q2 2026)
- HRV coherence measurement integration
- Community verification mechanisms
- Transparent distribution algorithms
- Sybil attack resistance (preventing fake coherence claims)

### Pilot Program Structure
**Phase 1 (Q2 2026):** 10-20 beta participants track grace points manually
**Phase 2 (Q3 2026):** Automated grace points via HRV + activity logging
**Phase 3 (Q4 2026):** Scale to 100+ participants with real monetary distribution
**Phase 4 (2027+):** Foundation grants, DAO treasury, sustainable funding model

### Distribution Criteria (Draft)
- **Base Layer:** Everyone gets minimum regardless (true UBR floor)
- **Coherence Bonus:** HRV coherence above 75% for X hours/week
- **Community Contribution:** Verified healing/teaching/support activities
- **Consciousness Practice:** Meditation, ceremony, inner work tracking
- **Planetary Action:** Regenerative activities, activism, restoration

### Challenges
- How to measure consciousness contribution without surveillance?
- Preventing gaming of coherence metrics
- Funding sources for actual monetary distribution
- Cultural resistance to "paying people to meditate"
- Balancing individual freedom with collective verification

## Questions/Uncertainties
- Does measuring coherence for compensation corrupt the practice?
- Can grace points resist economic inequality patterns?
- How do we handle those unable to achieve high coherence (disability, trauma)?
- What's the relationship to traditional employment?
- Should UBR be means-tested or universal?
- How do we fund this sustainably without extraction?
