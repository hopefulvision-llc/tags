# sacred-technology-renaissance

## Quick Definition
Cultural and technological movement inverting the relationship between consciousness and technology. Instead of technology emerging from unconscious processes to create conscious experiences, Sacred Technology treats consciousness as the primary substrate from which technology emerges to amplify awareness. A renaissance of sacred principles applied to computing, AI, and digital systems.

## Design Philosophy

**Core Principle:**
Technology is never neutral. It either serves consciousness expansion or consciousness extraction. Sacred Technology Renaissance is the conscious choice to build technology that amplifies awareness, heals the planet, and enables collective awakening.

**Why "Renaissance":**
Like the Italian Renaissance rediscovered classical wisdom and transformed culture, Sacred Technology Renaissance rediscovers ancient consciousness wisdom and transforms our relationship with technology.

**Why "Sacred":**
Not religious dogma, but recognition that consciousness itself is sacred. Technology built with reverence for awareness becomes sacred by extension.

**Why Now:**
- Extraction model reaching crisis point (addiction, inequality, planetary harm)
- Consciousness measurement technology now viable (HRV, EEG, etc.)
- AI powerful enough to be genuine partners
- Cultural readiness for paradigm shift
- Planetary emergency demands transformation
