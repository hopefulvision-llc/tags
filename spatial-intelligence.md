## Known Risks

**Risk 1: AR Dependency**
Users rely on overlays instead of developing natural spatial awareness

**Mitigation:** Progressive fading as awareness develops, teaching mode not permanent mode

**Risk 2: Visual Clutter**
Too many overlays obscure actual environment

**Mitigation:** Minimalist design, user control over what's visible, auto-fade

**Risk 3: Pseudoscience Perception**
Energy fields not scientifically validated, dismissed as woo

**Mitigation:** Correlate with measurable data (HRV coherence), present as models not absolute truth

**Risk 4: Safety Hazards**
Users distracted by AR while navigating physical space

**Mitigation:** Minimal interface, audio cues, safety warnings, pedestrian mode
